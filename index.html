Mission

Enable agents to transact safely, cheaply, and compliantly.

Autono is the policy & payments gateway for AI agents across crypto and traditional finance.

The problem

Agents pay for APIs/services in real time, but companies lack spend caps, vendor controls, and audit trails. Payments fragment across rails; gas/fees pile up; finance can‚Äôt trust or reimburse.

The solution

Agents never touch raw wallets or bank keys. They request short-lived policy tokens. Every payment is evaluated against rules, routed to the cheapest settlement rail, batched when possible, and returned with a signed receipt.

Cheaper rails, fewer failures

  • Route to lowest-cost rail (USDC, Lightning, bank)
  • Batch micro-tx to cut per-tx fees
  • Prevent duplicate/invalid spend before it settles
  • Signed receipts unlock reimbursement & chargeback
